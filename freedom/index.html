VALORANT is a free-to-play 5v5 tactical first-person shooter (FPS) developed by Riot Games.

In each match: One team attacks and tries to plant a device called the Spike. The other team defends and tries to stop the plant or defuse the Spike if it's planted. Teams switch sides halfway through the match. The first team to win 13 rounds wins the game (in standard competitive play). What makes VALORANT unique is that every character, called an agent, has special abilities. However, aiming, movement, teamwork, and strategy are still the most important skills.
